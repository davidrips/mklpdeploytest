-who we are-
We are a group of people who want to make education work better while giving students an opportunity to succeed in the challenging and competive modern market, before, during, and after college.


We want to connect students and parents to schools and businessses to deliver transparent collaborative opportunities.
-what we do-
Serving the untapped needs of the global pre-professional education market, myKlovr provides students, parents, teachers, schools, and businesses with a single source platform, which offers each user personalized, data-driven recommendations.

myKlōvr is a unique B2C and B2B platform providing students with a personalized 'return on identity'. Each student is assigned their own real-time MyKlovr score - KROI©, which is responsive to the student’s personal, academic, and professional achievements. In addition to serving as a more wholesome metric of a student's performance, the personal score can be used for preferential goods and services through the exclusive MyKlovr proprietary Education Exchange.
-how we do it-
myKlōvr gives users the ability to continually define, quantify and manage their very own academic, personal, and professional data assessments, and empowers them with continuous actionable and customized recommendations. Users have the ability to share these insights with friends, family, teachers, and businesses, while benefiting from the collective knowledge of their peers.

The myKlōvr Marketplace Exchange offers users recommended services such as access to tutors, mentors, and coaches, and personally selects relevant goods such as books, courses, and many other products, providing users with a customized blueprint for a success plan. The MyKlovr Marketplace functions as the transactional side of the platform; it offers seamless integration, and most importantly, true price transparency to and from all users.
