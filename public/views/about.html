-meet the team-
Gustavo Dolfino, Founder & CEO
Prior to myKlōvr, Gustavo was founder and CEO of WhiteRock Capital Partners, Inc and The WhiteRock Group, LLC. Before founding WhiteRock, Gustavo was a senior investment banker at UBS, Smith Barney and JE Capital. In addition to his career in investment banking, Gustavo founded Cancer Frontiers Inc. an internet based B2B provider of genetic oncology drugs. Mr. Dolfino is a frequent contributor on CNN and CNBC, he has an MBA from Thunderbird and a BBA from The George Washington University.
Jason Bowman, Global President & Co-Founder
Prior to Jason joining myKlōvr, he was the CEO and Co-Founder of My Weather Concierge, where he, along with other top tier weather forecasters provide in-depth up-to-the-minute forecasts. While Co-founding and leading MWC, Jason was the Senior Vice President of National Media Sales at SheKnows Media, the number-one women's lifestyle digital media company with 100 million unique visitors per month. Prior to SheKnows Media, Jason spent almost ten years representing the Millennial generation years at MTV Networks and VEVO. At MTV he was a Senior Account Executive for MTV, MTV2, MTV Digital and MTV3 where he oversaw strategic brand planning, upfront negotiations, and multimedia solutions across the US Ad Sales department.
Hui-Wen Wang, Chief Technology Officer
Hui-Wen is a digital technology leader in startups and large corporations. Prior to myKlōvr, he was a Senior Vice President at Citibank, where he led the technology vision, strategy, roadmap, enterprise architecture and development of mobile app, Internet portal, omni-channel digital marketing platform and content management solutions. In addition, Hui-Wen was a Director of Technology at Cablevision, where he provided leadership and managed the architecture and development of eCommerce and marketing sites. Prior to Cablevision, Hui-Wen was a CTO for an internet startup company. Hui-Wen holds a PhD degree in Physics and Master degree in Computer Science.
Piotr Jurjewicz ,Chief Strategy & Marketing Officer
Mr. Jurjewicz is myKlovr’s Chief Strategy and Business Development Officer. Prior to this position, Piotr was Vice President of Marketing and a Member of the Board for Bavaria S.A. (SABMiller, Colombia) in Bogota, Colombia, as well as Regional Consumer Marketing Director for SABMiller, Latin America, based in Miami FL. Piotr’s expertise includes, but is not limited to, market segmentation, growth strategy, portfolio development, as well as brand equity and media and communications. Mr. Jurjewicz has lived and worked in six countries on three continents, including Europe, Latin America and in the US. He has an MBA from Nottingham Trent University.
Fanny Heniene, Computational Data Scientist
A Graduate of Harvard University with a Master of Science (MSc), Computation Science and Engineering with a Concentration in Data Science and Statistical Computing, focusing upon visual representation of data and feature extraction of data.
David Rips, Head of Marketing
A graduate of Rutgers University and certified in Full Stack Development, David has worked on several mobile applications before joining myKlōvr. David is passionate about writing and marketing and is helping myKlõvr create a consistent and well integrated product.
Pedro Veintimilla, Account Executive
A Marketing and Management Information Systems graduate from Westminster College with a Masters in Communication Science from FLACSO. Prior to joining Myklovr, Pedro was the lead market researcher at Metropolitan Touring, the largest inbound tourism company in Ecuador and Galapagos. He also co-founded Chakrita Lab, a contained agriculture company which developed food security projects in Galapagos. Pedro has experience in digital advertising, product supply channels and entrepreneurship. Additionally, Pedro has lived on three different continents, giving him a unique multicultural background.
